About

Welcome to PETLY: Let's Save Stray Pups In Sri Lanka

At Petly, we believe in creating a haven for animals in need, a place where tails wag with joy and purrs fill the air. Our mission is simple: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals, providing them with a second chance at a happy and fulfilling life. Whether they come to us as strays, surrenders, or from overcrowded shelters, every furry friend at Petly receives the love, care, and attention they deserve. With a dedicated team of staff and volunteers, we strive to match each animal with their perfect forever home, ensuring a bond that lasts a lifetime. Join us in our commitment to making a difference, one paw at a time. Welcome to Petly, where every animal's story matters.
